About Talking Chloe

An AI girlfriend experience powered by cutting-edge language models, real-time 3D rendering, and computer vision — built with love for immersive conversations.

🤖

AI-Powered Brain

Chloe uses Groq's llama-3.3-70b model for ultra-fast, emotionally intelligent responses in Hindi, English, and Hinglish.

📹

Computer Vision

Real-time camera analysis detects your mood and clothing color — Chloe reacts to what she sees on your webcam!

🎭

3D Animations

Chloe is a fully rigged 3D model with idle breathing, blinking, lip sync, and action animations like hip-hop, catwalk, and kiss.

🌐

Multilingual

Switch between Hindi, English (IN), and English (US) during calls. Chloe replies in proper Hindi script — not just Hinglish!

🔒

Private by Design

Your chat history is stored locally in your own database. Login is optional — you can always chat as a guest.

⚡

Blazing Fast

Groq's LPU inference delivers responses in under a second, making conversations feel natural and real-time.
